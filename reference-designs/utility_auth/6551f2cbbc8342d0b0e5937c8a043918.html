Frequently Asked Questions

12 Articles
How do I start a visa application? expand_more

To begin your application, click on the 'Start Application' button on your main dashboard. You will be prompted to select your destination country and visa type. Our system will then generate a customized checklist of documents based on your specific requirements.

Is my personal data secure? expand_more

Security is our top priority. We use bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption to protect your data. All documents are stored on encrypted servers that comply with global privacy standards, including GDPR and CCPA. Your information is only accessible to authorized legal professionals assigned to your case.

What documents are required? expand_more

While requirements vary by visa type, common documents include a valid passport, proof of financial support, passport-sized photographs, and travel itinerary. Our platform provides a dynamic checklist that updates in real-time as you enter your details, ensuring you never miss a required item.

How long does the process take? expand_more

Standard processing typically takes 2-4 weeks for document preparation and review by our experts. Government processing times vary significantly by country (anywhere from 5 days to 6 months). You can track your application status in real-time through your VisaHelper dashboard.

Can I get a refund if my visa is denied? expand_more

While we cannot guarantee government approval, we offer a service fee refund policy for certain premium packages if denial occurs due to our error. Government fees themselves are non-refundable by the respective embassies. Please review our Terms of Service for specific refund criteria.
